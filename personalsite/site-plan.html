Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help anyone have a good guide through the national parks of Arizona and Utah, no matter how experienced they are. We offer tours for anyone from a family on a road trip making a stop along the way, to the most experienced climbers and hikers looking to explore in new ways. Our experienced guides are focused on giving our clients the best experience suited for their specific needs.

Audience

Our target audience is anyone who wants to visit a national park, but doesn't know where to start or just wants a simplified plan. We help them by making their plan as simple as possible

Content

Home page

Welcome to Grand Parks! We provide a simple way to plan your outdoor trips best suited for your needs. You can customize your trip to whatever degree you choose, you give us the ideas and we do the rest, or you can customise your trip to the finest detail. If you so choose, you can also choose to have a professional guide you on your trip, or we can provide a map with useful information for zero cost. We also provide a suggested list of equipment and supplies where you can use your own equipment, or we can provide a prebuilt kit. Good luck and have a good trip!

How It Works

Organizing a trip will be a series of steps consisting of details to help a user choose. The first step is choosing a location and time. This one is pretty much solely based on where the user wants to go, but a database of locations with tags could be added in the future. The second step is choosing a trail in the park theyâ€™d like to go to. These trails could have tags of features of the trails, like time to finish, difficulty of the trail, and pictures of the scenery. The third step is picking a guide. This step consists of a profile of each guide and the personality and experience to help the user choose. The fourth step uses a list attached to the trail to provide a list of equipment and supplies needed. Users can either use their own equipment or we can provide a kit for a price.
