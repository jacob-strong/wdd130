How It Works

1.

The first step is choosing a location and time. This part is pretty much up to where you want to go, we offer all of the main parks in Arizona and Utah, for more see the Locations tab.

2.

The Second part involves picking which trail you want to hike. This part is dependent on the level of difficulty you're comfortable with, you set your difficulty and we'll put together a list of every trail within your comfort range.

3.

The third and last part is putting together what you need for your journey. We have a list of suggested equipment and supplies for each trail you can customize for what you want, or we have a kit so you don't have to worry about it. You also have the option of having a professional trail guide to help you on your trip.
